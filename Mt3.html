What is
Background Design Art

Background design is a crucial component in visual art, whether it's in traditional painting, digital art, animation, or video games. It serves as the foundation upon which the main subjects and elements of the artwork are placed, giving context and enhancing the overall composition. While background design may seem like a secondary feature in some art forms, its influence on the mood, storytelling, and aesthetic appeal cannot be understated.
